I'm Jianbang LIU, a PhD student from Robotics, Perception & AI Lab in the Department of Electronic Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I graduated from Master of Science program provided by the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The University of Hong Kong, and a joint bachelor program provided by The Hong Kong Polytechnic University and The Sun Yat-Sen University.

Currently, My research interests include trajectory prediction, feasible region prediction for path planning, multi-modal data fusion, robotic perception for navigation, and autonomous driving.
